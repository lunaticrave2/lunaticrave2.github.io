Setup

A detailed guide on setting up LR2, written by auric, can be found here. A quick summary of the installation process is outlined below:

  1. Extract LR2.zip to any location on your hard drive
  2. Ensure your system locale is set to Japanese
  3. Open LR2.exe
  4. Enter a username and password to be used for your LR2IR account
  5. Navigate to the JUKEBOX1 tab and drag in the folder containing your BMS files
  6. Click Play
